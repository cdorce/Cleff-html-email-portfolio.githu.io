A little bit about me

My name is Cleffton, I’m currently living in Ecuador.

I’m a creative person, when I’m not creating digital designs I enjoy spending time playing the piano and I love traveling (I’ve lived in 4 different countries in the past 10 years). I speak Spanish, English and French fluently.
I have a bachelor’s degree in business administration and started a master's in human resources just to find out that was more interested in expressing my ideas with colors and shapes. I started working as a general marketing specialist back then in 2017.

I work with several businesses/organizations from Europe and USA. I used to be in charge of email marketing for a company based in the UK, I worked alongside the CEO to come up with ideas and my main job was reaching out to influencers via social media, designing email templates, and following up.
I worked as an SEO specialist for a blogger from Australia, I did some graphic design/social media marketing for a non-profit organization in the USA and some freelancing landing page design. In the past 4 years I’ve been using tools like wordPress, mailchimp, procreate, photoshop, adobe XD and Illustrator…
